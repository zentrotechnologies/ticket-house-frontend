Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 01 February 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all ticket purchases made on Ticket House, operated by Zentro Technologies LLP.

1. General Policy

All ticket purchases are generally non-refundable, unless:

  • The event is cancelled by the organizer.
  • The event is officially postponed and a refund is approved.
  • Refund is mandated by applicable law.

2. Event Cancellation

If an event is cancelled:

  • Users will receive a full refund of the ticket price.
  • Convenience/service fees may not be refunded depending on organizer policy.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7–14 business days.

3. Event Postponement

If an event is postponed:

  • Tickets will remain valid for the new date.
  • Refund requests may be allowed if announced by the organizer.
  • Refund timelines may vary.

4. User-Initiated Cancellation

Tickets cancelled by the user are typically non-refundable.

Exceptions may apply if:

  • The organizer explicitly allows cancellation.
  • Cancellation occurs within a specified window (if applicable).

5. Duplicate Payments

If duplicate payments occur due to technical error:

  • The extra amount will be refunded within 7–10 business days after verification.

6. Failed Transactions

If payment is debited but ticket not confirmed:

  • Amount will automatically be reversed by the bank within 5–7 business days.
  • If not received, users may contact support.

7. Force Majeure

No refunds shall be provided for cancellations due to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government restrictions
  • Acts of God
  • Pandemic-related restrictions

Unless the organizer issues refunds.

8. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.

Processing time depends on:

  • Payment gateway
  • Bank processing timelines

For refund-related concerns:
